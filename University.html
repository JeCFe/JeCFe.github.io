My University Experience

At university I have already completed my foundation year (first class pass) and my first year of actual degree (awaiting final grade). I will be moving onto my second year in the new academic session.

My time out of my studies has been focused on enhancing my program development skills by developing personal projects. Notably a basic console line password manager; this manager can handle multiple user using verification methods to ensure a user can only access their passwords, each password is encrypted using a symmetrical encryption algorithm, and then decrypts to display the password to the user.

While my time at the university has not be completely enveloped in my studies or development, I had joined societies to occupy my time. With my intentions to join additional societies in later years.
